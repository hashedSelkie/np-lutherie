Services

Repair and restoration

As a violin maker I am passionate about instruments and their history, because they influence my own new instruments that I build in the workshop.

If you have an instrument that needs to be repaired or fully restored, please don’t hesitate to make an appointment.

The initial appointment is almost like a doctor's appointment: I shall evaluate what you and your  instrument need.

After that there is a conversation about the total cost and once that has the clients  greenlight, I shall start the work.

Payment plans for more extensive repairs and restoration can be  discussed beforehand as well.
