Handmade violin by Oliver Nicholson 4/4

This stunning violin was made by the talented Oliver Nicholson during his time as a student at the world renowned Newark School of Violin Making.

Based on the Huberman model by Stradivarius, Oliver adjusted the shape creating this striking shape.

Oil varnish finish and professionally set up: this instrument has a powerful sound that responds easily to the player. A perfect instrument if you're looking to upgrade to a handmade violin: ideal for the musician going to conservatoir.

Oliver is a young violin maker based in Ely England and a master of many crafts. He also makes beautiful handmade violin and viola bows and he engineers intricate specialist tools for luthiers. With a keen eye to detail Oliver produces wonderful instruments with clean and crisp woodwork. Combining traditional building techniques and the best tonewood he manages to create instruments that are not only beautiful to look at, they also sound and play amazing.

Priced at £3000 and available to take on trial.
